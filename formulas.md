# RLC Circuit Formulas

## Series RLC Circuit

### Resonant Angular Frequency
\[
\omega_0 = \frac{1}{\sqrt{LC}}
\]

### Resonant Frequency
\[
f_0 = \frac{1}{2\pi\sqrt{LC}}
\]

### Quality Factor
\[
Q = \frac{1}{R}\sqrt{\frac{L}{C}}
\]

### Damping Coefficient
\[
\alpha = \frac{R}{2L}
\]

## Damping Cases

- Underdamped: \(\alpha < \omega_0\)
- Critically damped: \(\alpha = \omega_0\)
- Overdamped: \(\alpha > \omega_0\)
